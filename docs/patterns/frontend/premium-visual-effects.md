# Premium Visual Effects Playbook

Use premium effects to enhance hierarchy, not to distract.

## Approved Effect Families

- Gradient mesh or spotlight backgrounds
- Glass/translucent surface cards
- Soft glow emphasis around critical CTA blocks
- Subtle texture/noise overlays

## Performance and Accessibility Boundaries

- Preserve readable contrast on top of gradients and overlays.
- Avoid effect stacks that trigger high paint costs on scroll.
- Keep visual enhancements optional in reduced-motion contexts.
- Ensure decorative layers are ignored by assistive tech.

## Design Rule of Thumb

Choose one dominant premium effect per section. Combine only when there is clear visual purpose.
