# Design and Media QA Checklist

Use this checklist when applying P64 design/media enhancements.

## Visual Quality

- Hero and CTA hierarchy remains clear at all breakpoints.
- Premium effects support content, not visual noise.
- Typography and spacing remain consistent with the base system.

## Media Quality

- SVGs are optimized and accessible.
- Decorative graphics do not reduce content contrast.
- Key media assets are properly sized and named.

## Motion Quality

- Motion effects are wrapped with reduced-motion safeguards.
- Hover and entrance effects are subtle and performant.

## Evidence

- Include before/after screenshots for at least one hero and one CTA.
- Keep command output and checklist notes with the QA evidence package.
